Shallow Fried King Fish

  • Time: 20
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
King Fish 300 gms
Ginger garlic juice 1 tbsp
Lemon Juice 1 tbsp
Chilly powder 1 tbsp
Turmeric powder 1 tbsp
Roasted coriander powder 1 tbsp
Salt 1 tbsp or per taste
Oil 3 tbsp

Step 1: Wash the fish and pat dry with kitchen towel..

Step 2: In a bowl add all the ingredients except oil and make a smooth paste..

Step 3: Rub the masala paste all over the fish and marinate it for sometime. the longer time the better..

Step 4: In a pan heat the oil. Shallow fry the fish for 5-6 mins each on both sides in medium heat. Remove and serve as it is or onion, chilly and lemon slices..

Finger Chips

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Big sized potato ( finger chopped) 3
Oil 200gm for deep fry
Salt 1/2 tsp
Black pepper 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Take a container , add 4 cup water in it and boil finger chopped potatoes in it

Step 2: Now take it out and keep in fridge for marinate

Step 3: Now heat oil in a pan and deep fry potatos

Step 4: now it's ready ..serve it

Breakfast Smoothie

  • Time: 3
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Yellow banana: 1 nos
Oats 2 tbsp
Almond milk / Diary milk: 1/2 cups
Chickoo 1 nos
Blueberry jam 1 tsp
Beetroot powder 1 tsp
Flax seed powder 1 tsp
Chia seeds 1 tsp

Step 1: Blend together all the ingredients except chia seeds into fine consistency.

Step 2:Top it with chia seeds and serve.
